The Creamy Coconut Carrot Curry

Ingredients

  • carrots - similar sized small pieces
  • full fat coconut milk
  • Onion
  • Turmeric
  • Coriander
  • Curry leaves
  • Cardamom (optional)
  • salt to taste

Recipe

  • 01. Peel the carrots, rinse thoroughly, and cut them into small pieces.
  • 02. Chop the onions and cut the green chilies into slices. Take some curry leaves as well.
  • 03. Prepare the turmeric powder and salt. Prepare the coconut milk as well.
  • 04. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Combine the carrot and cooking oil in a mixing bowl. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes, covered, on medium heat.
  • 05.Stir in the onion, curry leaves, and green chilies. Cook, uncovered, over medium heat until carrots are tender. To avoid burning, stir occasionally. It takes me about 2 minutes.
  • 06. Finally, stir in the turmeric powder, salt, and coconut milk. Make a good mixture.
  • 07. Cook, covered, for 1 to 2 minutes on medium heat.
  • 08. Taste the curry and adjust salt if needed.
